About Me

Hi! I'm Tanner Helms, a passionate visual storyteller currently based in the beautiful state of Alaska. With a deep appreciation for the art of capturing life's most beautiful moments, I specialize in capturing weddings, commercials, outdoor adventures, and special events. With each click of the shutter, my goal is to capture your most cherished memories. I want to put you right back to those amazing moments, whether it's the sweet cuddles of newlyweds, the mind-blowing views of Alaska's outdoors, or the fun celebrations of life's important milestones.

Let's work together to capture your significant moment! What sets me apart from other photographers and videographers is my dedication to building a connection with my clients. I don't just arrive and take random shots. I invest time in getting to know you, ensuring that your unique personality and character shine through in every picture and video I capture!
